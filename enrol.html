Sign up:

The Lifetime Membership fee for new students is £12.00, it’s a one off fee paid prior to your 1st class (includes your SA top for Early Dance, Modern and Tap students only).

Course Fees:

From £5.50 per class, payments are termly, credit card online, cash or cheques to Aretha Marques, or directly into our bank account. Fees are non-refundable; if the student misses a class a makeup lesson can be booked within a month according to availability. All fees are to be paid in advance. If fees are not paid by the second lesson of the term a £15.00 late fee will be applied. Drop in is only available for adult classes: £10 per class paid before the class starts, if not signing up for the term the dancer will not be considered a regular SA Dance student therefore will not be invited to take part in performances. Payment due dates if paying for Half Term: 1st day of the term and 2nd payment before half term on May 21st Wednesday, 22nd Thursday and 24thSaturday.

Taster Session:

Book a taster session at SA Dance School for only £6.00, children classes only, please email info@sa-dance.co.uk to check availability. Offer is only valid for dancers that have never attended a class at SA Dance School before.
We welcome new students throughout the term, please email for availability. For new dancers joining after the start of term, payment will be for the remaining number of weeks.

Uniform:

Tiny Steps – comfortable clothes, ballet shoes or bare foot for child and the adult.
Ballet - light pink leotard and skirt, light pink ballet shoes and light pink tights.
Contemporary, Modern, Jazz, Early Dance, Tap – black leggings/jogging pants, black dance shoes/barefoot/ballet shoes/tap shoes and SA Dance top.
Hair tied up in a bun or ponytail. You can purchase the correct ballet and modern shoes from us.

Please note: for health and safety reasons Parents are not allowed inside the studios during the lessons unless they are taking part in the lesson, for example at the Tiny Steps class. It is the responsibility of each parent to contact SA Dance when the child is absent and also to inform us of any medical issues or special needs, two unannounced absences may jeopardise your child’s place in the class. Tardiness is not appreciated at SA Dance and will be addressed if persists. Photos and Filming: We do take photos and film our students in class, in special events and during performances; these will be showcased in SA Dance events, SA Facebook page, SA website and newsletters.
